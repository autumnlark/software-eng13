About Us

Server Monk Software is a company started by a group of seven 3rd year Heriott Watt students based in Edinburgh. As a company we aim to create high quality and fantastic looking software products for any client and their business.

Every member of our team brings their own unique skill set, that when combined with the talent of the rest of the company allows us to bring to life creative and well-designed projects that achieves our clients highest expectations.

No matter what idea you have for either a software project or website, we will always want to listen and discover if we can be the company that can bring your creations to life!
