Server Monk Software Privacy Policy

What data do we collect?

Server Monk Software will collect the following personally identifiable information:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide Server Monk Software with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Submit the form located in our Contact Us page.

How will we use your data?

Server Monk Software collects your data so that we can:

  • Reply to business enquiries made by you.

Server Monk Software does not share your data with third parties.

How do we store your data?

Server Monk Software stores your data on an Amazon Web Services virtual server instance hosted in London. Your data will only be accessible to our automated email responder and the customer service agent assigned to your query. Your data is encrypted at rest and any Server Monk Software employees will only be able to access the data with the correct credentials, in this case a RSA 4096 bit private key. Password access to your data is not allowed to prevent compromised passwords being unintentionally used during data storage by Server Monk Software employees.

Server Monk Software will keep your first name, last name, and email address for 1 year. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data by running an automated script which removes all enquiries in our database which are made 1 year prior to the date the script is executed.

What are your data protection rights?

Server Monk Software would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request Server Monk Software for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that Server Monk Software correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Server Monk Software to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that Server Monk Software erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that Server Monk Software restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to Server Monk Software’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that Server Monk Software transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: info@servermonk.software

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How do we use cookies?

Server Monk Software does not use any cookies, however, our Distributed Denial-of-Service protection provider/DNS host Cloudflare does place cookies in your browser to identify you as a human user.

What types of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:

  • Functionality – Server Monk Software uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

Privacy policies of other websites

Server Monk Software keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 9 January 2019.
